How To Complete Your Purchase Using SECURE-eBill
 
1.Select the SECURE-eBill as your method of payment during checkout.
2.An eBill for your order will be sent to your email address.
3.Login to your online banking system to pay your eBill. You may be required to setup the merchant information within your banking profile to complete the payment.
Note: Pease allow 2-4 business days for banks to process and transfer payment.

As soon as your payment has been processed you will receive a receipt by email and your goods will be shipped.
